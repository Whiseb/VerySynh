# FAQ

## How is VerySynh different from RAG?
RAG adds text to the prompt. The model can ignore it. VerySynh's memory constrains generation through orchestrator rules. Memory is not advisory.

## Does VerySynh need a large model?
No. The intelligence is in the memory, not the weights.

## How does the system learn without fine-tuning?
It grows and crystallizes the memory. The model weights do not change.

## What happens if no pattern is found?
The system says: "I have no verified method. Here is my best hypothesis, but I am not certain."

## Can VerySynh work without a Unifier?
No. Structural normalization is mandatory.

## Does VerySynh solve creative tasks?
No. For unique creative tasks, it falls back to standard LLM behavior and honestly reports uncertainty.

## What domains are supported?
Architecture is domain-agnostic. Initial focus: programming. Medical, legal, engineering require specialized Unifiers.
